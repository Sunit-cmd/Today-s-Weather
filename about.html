About Today's Weather

Our Mission

At Today's Weather, we believe that staying informed about the world around you should be as beautiful as it is functional. Our mission is to provide accurate, real-time weather data wrapped in a stunning, modern interface that makes checking the forecast a delight.

Key Features

  • ✨ Real-time Updates: Instant access to current conditions for any city worldwide.
  • 📅 3-Day Forecast: Plan your week ahead with our precise short-term predictions.
  • 🔍 Global Search: Search from thousands of locations across the globe.
  • 🌈 Dynamic UI: Our interface adapts to the current weather, reflecting the atmosphere of your location.

The Technology

Today's Weather is built using modern web standards including HTML5, CSS3 with advanced backdrop-filters, and vanilla JavaScript. We leverage the powerful WeatherAPI to ensure you always have the most up-to-date atmospheric information at your fingertips.
